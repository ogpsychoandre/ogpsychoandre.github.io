"Son Of My Father"

VideoClip Identity Mixed Media

A project developed as part of the "Audiovisual Production" course in the second year of my undergraduate degree.

An exploration of lineage and self — what we carry from those who came before us, and how the body becomes an archive of inherited gestures, silences, and unspoken histories.

"Son Of My Father" moves between documentary instinct and visual abstraction, blending intimate footage with painted and layered imagery. A meditation on fatherhood, continuity, and the weight of a name.
