I'm André Silva and I'm finishing my degree in Design and Multimedia in UC. I'm curious and always looking for new challenges. I love digital media, audiovisual and multimedia. My work centers on individuality, celebrating the beauty of what makes each person, story, and perspective distinct.

I move fluidly between hands-on processes and digital execution, building layered mixed media compositions, developing striking brand identities, directing and editing motion pieces, and crafting dynamic visual systems that live across print and screen.
