"The Old Room"

Interactive Multimedia Three.js MediaPipe

"Together we write, Together we listen, Together we fill this room."

An immersive experience built around sensory memory and nostalgia. The user enters an old room and relives the past through invisible, organic interactions — where the body becomes the interface.

Developed with Three.js for 3D rendering and MediaPipe for real-time gesture detection, the project transforms physical hand movements into digital commands across three distinct objects: a record player, a typewriter, and a Nintendo console.

The Record Player

Three distinct vinyls, each triggering a different audio track. MediaPipe detects a closed fist to grab the tonearm — releasing it starts the music. An open hand controls the scratch.

Sensory Memory
02

The Typewriter

A fully functional typewriter inside the 3D space. Each key pressed replicates the mechanics of physical writing on a virtual page — including the lateral carriage return, triggered by hand gesture.

03

Nintendo Console

An 8-bit game of "Malmequer" (Love me, love me not). A pinch gesture pulls each petal — completing a full cycle from intro to final result, with visual and audio feedback.
